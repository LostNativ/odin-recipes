Stiff Pap

BThis stiff pap is a great accompaniment to stews and braai meat.


Ingredients

  • 3 cups water
  • 2 cups maize meal
  • 2 T butter
  • Water

Steps to Make

  1. Ready medium pot
  2. Heat stove to medium heat
  3. bring water to a boil
  4. add maize meal and stiff every 5 min for 15min
  5. add Butter
